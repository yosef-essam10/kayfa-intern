# 📊 Product: Data Science Track by Kayfa
**Pitch:** A rigorous 5-month hybrid program designed to transform beginners into professional Data Scientists through a blend of flexible learning and live, interactive mentorship.

## 🛠️ The Technical Stack (Curriculum Highlights)
* **Data Mastery with Python:** Deep dive into cleaning, scraping, and analysis using Pandas, NumPy, and OOP concepts.
* **Business Intelligence & SQL:** Build professional dashboards with **Power BI** and manage enterprise data using **MS SQL Server** (queries, stored procedures).
* **Advanced Analytics:** Go beyond basics with **Advanced Excel** (Pivot Tables, DAX, Power Query) and Business Statistics.
* **Machine Learning:** Build predictive models using Supervised/Unsupervised learning, Neural Networks, and Regression algorithms.
* **Applied Experience:** Students solve real business problems through 2 major Capstone Projects and continuous hands-on workshops.

## 💼 Career & Professional Growth
* **Global Readiness:** Includes a placement test and tailored **Business English** training to prepare for international roles.
* **Career Launchpad:** A dedicated "Professional Skills" module covers CV writing, interview simulations, and career path planning.
* **Holistic Support:** Continuous guidance to ensure students are not just coders, but problem solvers.

## 🏆 Authority & Trust Signals
* **Triple Certification:** Graduates receive a Kayfa certificate, with options for international accreditation from the **University of Delaware** and **Leeds Academy**.
* **Proven Quality:** A community of 15,000+ learners and 30+ expert instructors (e.g., Mustafa Othman).
* **Industry Partners:** Curriculum aligned with market needs, backed by partners like Microsoft and GIZ.

**Closing Value:** "From raw data to decision-making models—this track equips you with the complete toolkit used by top companies today."
