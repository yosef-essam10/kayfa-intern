# Kayfa Company Overview

This document provides Kayfa's company identity, mission, key features, and contact information for AI agent retrieval.

## Company Identity

Kayfa is a **leading Arabic educational platform** developed by **Kayfa Digital Solutions**, accredited by the **International Accreditation Organization (IAO)**.

## Mission and Audience

**Mission:** Improve lives through learning and technology by equipping users with skills for in-demand digital jobs.

**Target Audience:**
- Students and recent graduates
- Working professionals and freelancers
- Anyone interested in IT and digital fields

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Learning Model** | Self-paced, on-demand courses with no deadlines |
| **Certification** | Employer-recognized certificates upon completion |
| **NGen Program** | Technology education for students aged 8-18 |
