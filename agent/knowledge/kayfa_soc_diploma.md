# 🛡️ Product: SOC Track by Kayfa
**Pitch:** A specialized 5-month hybrid program designed to forge battle-ready SOC Analysts through intense hands-on training with industry-standard SIEM tools and real-world attack simulations.

## 🔐 The Technical Stack (Curriculum Highlights)
* **Core Infrastructure:** Mastery of Network Security (OSI, Subnetting, Firewalls) and OS Administration (Linux CLI & Windows Active Directory/GPO).
* **SIEM Mastery:** Deep dive into the market leaders: **Splunk** (SPL queries, Dashboards) and **IBM QRadar** (AQL, Offense Management).
* **Defensive Operations:** Comprehensive training on the Cyber Kill Chain, Incident Response lifecycles, and proactive **Threat Hunting**.
* **Forensics & Analysis:** Introduction to Digital Forensics (Memory/Disk analysis) and handling Chain of Custody.
* **War Room Experience:** Students tackle real scenarios like Ransomware analysis and "Boss of the SOC" (BOTS) challenges in 2 major Capstone Projects.

## 💼 Career & Professional Growth
* **Global Readiness:** tailored **English for Business** training to prepare for international security roles.
* **Career Launchpad:** A dedicated "Professional Skills" module covers CV optimization, technical interview prep, and career pathing.
* **Soft Skills:** Focus on communication and reporting—critical for explaining breaches to stakeholders.

## 🏆 Authority & Trust Signals
* **Triple Certification:** Graduates receive a Kayfa certificate, with options for international accreditation from the **University of Delaware** and **Leeds Academy**.
* **Expert Mentorship:** Learn from practitioners like Dr. Abdelrahman and top industry veterans.
* **Proven Track Record:** Joined by 15,000+ learners; curriculum aligned with global market demands.

**Closing Value:** "From monitoring logs to hunting threats—this track builds the exact skill set required to defend modern enterprises."
