# Kayfa Policies and FAQs

This document covers Kayfa's subscription, refund, cancellation policies, and frequently asked questions.

## Subscription Policy

| Topic | Policy |
|-------|--------|
| **Term** | Annual basis only, auto-renews unless canceled |
| **Payment** | Online via bank transaction or credit card |
| **Pricing** | Subject to change; may vary by country/currency |
| **Cancellation** | Cancel before next billing date; takes effect at cycle end |

## Refund Policy

### General
No refunds once subscription fees have been withdrawn.

### Live/Onsite Courses
| Timing | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| 7+ days before start | Full refund |
| Less than 7 days before | 80% refund (20% admin fee) |
| After program started | No refund |

### Recorded Courses
| Access Status | Refund |
|---------------|--------|
| No content accessed | Full refund |
| Content accessed/downloaded | No refund |

### Processing
Refunds issued to original payment method within **7-30 working days**.

## Course Replacement

Users can replace a course with one of **equal or lower value** before the course begins. Pay difference if replacement is higher value.

## Frequently Asked Questions

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **What is Kayfa?** | Educational platform offering high-quality courses to improve lives through learning and technology. |
| **How do I access courses?** | On-demand via desktop/laptop. Access via confirmation email link or "My Learning" page after login. |
| **Are there deadlines?** | No deadlines to begin or complete courses. |
| **Can I preview courses?** | Yes, some courses offer free previews. |
| **Where can I get help?** | Email support@kayfa.io |
