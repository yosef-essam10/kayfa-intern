# Kayfa Privacy Policy

This document summarizes the key aspects of Kayfa's Privacy Policy, focusing on data collection, usage, and user rights, optimized for AI agent retrieval.

## Data Controller and Purpose

Kayfa is the **data controller**, responsible for deciding how personal information is collected, stored, and used [1]. The purpose of data collection is to provide services, communicate with users, troubleshoot issues, secure against fraud, improve services, analyze usage, serve personalized advertising, and comply with legal requirements [1].

## Information Collected

The following personal data is collected upon account registration and use of services [1]:

*   **Identity and Contact:** Name, Email Address, Year of Birth, Gender, Country of Residence/Origin, Phone Numbers, Social Media Accounts (if used for login).
*   **Usage Data:** Records of visits to Kayfa and engagement with educational content (pages, videos, assessments, posts, comments, and other interactions).
*   **Technical Data:** Information collected through automated means like cookies and web beacons to enable site functionality, remember preferences, and secure the site [1].

## User Rights

Users have several rights regarding their personal data [1]:

*   **Right to Access:** Request access to personal data held and information about its processing.
*   **Right to Rectification:** Request correction or update of inaccurate or incomplete information.
*   **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of personal data in certain circumstances.
*   **Right to Restrict Processing:** Request restriction of processing under certain conditions.
*   **Right to Object:** Object to the processing of personal data (e.g., for direct marketing).

To exercise these rights, users should contact Kayfa at **info@kayfa.io** [1].
